Enable Internet Connection Sharing of your current Network Adapter.

1. go to "Control Panel"
2. Click "Network and sharing Center"
3. Click "Change Adapter Settings"
4. Find out your current network adapter by which you are using internet.
5. Select and right click to open its propertise.
6. Click "Sharing"
7. Under "Internet Connection Sharing" check mark the "Allow other network users to connect through this computer's
   internet connection"
8. Apply or Ok.
9. Now open the Bilai-Fi and try to connect.
